In our third year, we are required to complete a capstone project. This capstone project is an important part of our curriculum, designed to give us the opportunity to apply the knowledge and skills we have gained throughout our studies in a practical and meaningful way.

The purpose of the capstone project is to solve real-world problems by developing a complete and functional system. By working on the capstone project as a team, we aim to gain hands-on experience in managing a real project from start to finish and to prove our ability to collaborate and deliver a quality system that meets specific needs.

### 1.1.1  Objective of Capstone

The main objective of our capstone project is to provide a comprehensive academic experience where we apply and integrate the knowledge and skills acquired throughout our degree program. This includes concepts from various subjects such as software engineering, system analysis, database management, and project management. By combining these disciplines, we aim to demonstrate our ability to develop practical and effective solutions.

### 1.1.2  Duration of Capstone

Our capstone project lasts for 6 months across two main phases: **Capstone I** and **Capstone II**. In this report, we focus on the activities completed during Capstone I.

We conduct the capstone project over a defined academic period, beginning on **1st October 2025** and concluding on **17th December 2025**, with a total duration of approximately 10 to 12 weeks for Capstone I. This period is structured to allow us sufficient time for planning, development, and evaluation.

The project duration is divided into several major phases, including requirement analysis, system design, development, testing, and documentation. Throughout these phases, we establish specific milestones and evaluation checkpoints to monitor our progress and ensure alignment with project objectives. This structured timeline supports effective project management and helps us successfully complete the capstone project within the allocated timeframe.
